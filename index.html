The story

Wandering with purpose

"I left my office job with a one-way ticket to Lisbon. That was five years ago."

What started as a solo backpacking trip turned into a calling. I spend my days exploring markets at 7am, getting lost in medieval streets, and sharing honest, unfiltered travel stories with a community that believes the best experiences happen when you slow down and look properly.

I'm not here to show you the Instagram-perfect version of Europe. I want to show you the real thing — the Sunday flea markets, the local trattorias with no English menus, the train journeys that take twice as long but show you everything.
